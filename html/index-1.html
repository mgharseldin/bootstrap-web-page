My Short Bio

Simply Put- I love to Create Things!

Ever since I was a little child, my imagination would go wild. I would not only paint on almost anything I could get my hands on but, I would create sculptures and while most girls have thier barbies enacting pool parties, mine were attending art galleries! I love to find the beauty in anything. Whether it be on the web, or in our daily life.

The idea of taking something ordinary and creating something extradanary. That moment where someone looks at your work and you get that smile from them like they have just received a present, that moment is priceless. To be able to have someone communicate what they want to you and for you to be able to bring it to life for them has always been such a rewarding part of my career!

My Experience

I have been fortunate to work with many different talented people in several organizations and non-profits as well, and with all of this various experience- one fact always remains "Creativity Comes in All Forms"and some people have it in thier minds and simply need help with us bringing thier form to life.

From printing projects such as something as basic as business cards to building size banners on grand openings to buidling someone's first dream of owning thier own online business, I have had the pleasure of doing it all!
