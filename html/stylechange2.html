About Me

Creativity is Intelligence-having fun!

I will admit there was a time in my life, where I would rather have had someone hand me a paint brush and a side of thier building and say"Have at it and create me something magical". However, one birthday years back my husband introduced me to the most important tool that helped me to change my mind quickly. A Waecom drawing tablet. Soon my ah ha moment appeared and I realized we can do amazing things with manipulations and animations to our art work. That yes, old school is fun and sometimes there is nothing like getting our hands dirty and painting but, the real magic lies within the boundless possibility of one's creativity and what they can do with today's ever growing technology.
